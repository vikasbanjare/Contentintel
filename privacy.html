Privacy Policy

ContentIntel · contentintel.in · Last updated 14 June 2026

ContentIntel ("we", "the service") helps creators check scripts, thumbnails, titles and ads. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it. ContentIntel is currently in private alpha.

What we collect

  • Account info you provide at sign-up: email, name, and optionally phone, the type of creator you are, and your main platform.
  • Usage counts: how many checks you've run (to apply your plan limit). We do not keep a copy of the scripts, thumbnails, titles or ads you submit — that content is sent for analysis and not stored on our servers.
  • Feedback you choose to send us.
  • Technical basics needed to keep you logged in (a session token in your browser's local storage).

How your content is processed

When you run a check, your submitted text or image is sent to our AI provider, Anthropic (the Claude API), to generate the analysis, and the result is returned to you. We do not sell your content and we do not use it to train models. Anthropic processes it under its own API terms and does not train on API data.

Who we share data with (sub-processors)

  • Supabase — stores your account and usage data.
  • Anthropic — processes the content you submit to produce your report.
  • Cloudflare — runs the secure proxy between the app and the AI.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the website.

We never sell your personal data.

Cookies & storage

We use your browser's local storage to keep you signed in and remember basic preferences (like light/dark mode). We don't use third-party advertising trackers.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account data at any time by emailing us. Deleting your account removes your profile and usage records.
